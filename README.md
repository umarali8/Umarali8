## 👋 About

I am a Data Scientist focused on translating ambiguous business problems into structured, data-driven solutions. My work spans the full analytics lifecycle — from raw data acquisition and cleaning, through statistical modeling and machine learning, to executive-facing reporting.

I hold myself to one standard: every model, dashboard, or analysis must connect to a business outcome — revenue, retention, efficiency, or risk — not just a metric on a slide.

**Core strengths**

| Area | What it means in practice |
|---|---|
| **Data Science** | End-to-end pipelines: ingestion, cleaning, feature engineering, modeling, deployment. |
| **Machine Learning** | Supervised and unsupervised methods applied to classification, regression, and segmentation problems. |
| **Business Intelligence** | Executive dashboards and reporting systems that reduce time-to-insight. |
| **Problem Solving** | Hypothesis-driven analysis applied to ambiguous, real-world business questions. |
| **Continuous Learning** | Active study of Generative AI, LLMs, and applied MLOps practices. |
| **Business Impact** | Work is scoped and evaluated against measurable KPIs, not technical novelty alone. |
